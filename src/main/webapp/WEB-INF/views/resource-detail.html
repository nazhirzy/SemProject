Depression

Depression is a common mental health disorder characterized by persistent sadness, loss of interest, and interference with daily life including sleep, appetite, and fatigue. It is a treatable condition resulting from biological, psychological, and social factors.

Key Characteristics

  • Persistent sadness: Intense feelings of emptiness lasting at least two weeks
  • Loss of interest: Diminished ability to find pleasure in once-enjoyable activities
  • Physical symptoms: Changes in sleep patterns, appetite, and energy levels
